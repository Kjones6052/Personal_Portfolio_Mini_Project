Introduction

I was born and raised in Southern California, and after my parents divorced when I was young I spent a few weeks a year visiting my mom in Connecticut. While growing up I really enjoyed playing soccer, and I played soccer until an incident occurred when I was 17. The incident was almost losing my right thumb in a landscaping accident, where a landscaper was trimming a bush and as I tried to walk around him he turned towards me and clipped my hand with the trimmer cutting thumb and severing a tendon. After high school I had several different jobs which lead me to selling real estate, and eventually starting my journey into coding.

Education

I never graduated from high school, in fact after dropping out of a Los Osos High School in the city of Rancho Cucamonga, CA I enrolled in a self study program called Options For Youth. I also got dropped from Options For Youth after a few months, and in 2009 I got my GED from Chaffey Adult School in Ontario, CA. After obtaining the GED I attended Chaffey Community College in Rancho Cucamonga for a little under 2 years before I quit attending and decided to work full time.

Work Experience

I first started working in 2005 when I got hired at a pizza restuarant, and it was there that I learned how to make pizza from start to finish. After a couple of months there I had spent time working in several different positions, including making the dough and sauce for the restuarant. After working in food I had a short term in retail working as a replenishment associate. I then started working in real estate as a broker's assistant before working my way up to team manager where I lead a team of agents and support personnel. I managed all the operations of the team from lead generation, contact relationship management, implementing automation and telemarketing, and coordinating transactions to ensure proper data and file management. In 2017 I obtained my real estate salesperson license and continue to work in that industry today.

Career Goals

After completing my education in software engineering, I plan to search for and obtain a job as a software engineer where I can utilize the tools and abilities I've aquired during my education. I look forward to a long career in this industry and I am excited to continue my journey in this field.
